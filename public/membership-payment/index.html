Complete Your Membership

Thank you for applying! Follow the steps below to complete your FBL membership.

01

Pay the One-Time Assessment Fee

The first step is completing the FBL assessment fee. This is a one-time payment and is required before moving forward with membership.

Pay Assessment Fee
02

Start Your Monthly Membership

Once your assessment fee has been paid, the next step is setting up your monthly FBL membership subscription ($150/month). This subscription will bill automatically each month.

Start Monthly Membership ($150/month)
03

We Confirm Your Membership

After your subscription is successfully set up, we will receive confirmation by email. Once that is confirmed, our team will activate your access inside the private FBL Circle community.

Please allow a short time for us to process your setup. Once your access is granted, you'll receive an email to activate your account.

If you already have the Circle app downloaded, simply open it when you receive your invitation, activate your account, and complete your profile. A profile photo is required to fully activate your membership.

04

Download the Circle App

While you wait for your access email, go ahead and download the Circle Communities app. Once we send your invitation, you'll be able to log in and access the FBL community.

iOS App Android App
