FBL Covenant

This is the short-form covenant for application and onboarding. It keeps the important part front and center without turning the process into a mile-long form.

What FBL commits to

FBL exists to develop leaders of conviction who pursue wholeness in faith, work, and life. Facilitators commit to intentional leadership formation, honest feedback, meaningful relationships, spiritual and strategic support, and a standard that calls members upward.

What members commit to

Members commit to integrity, confidentiality, consistency, contribution, accountability, direct communication, and full engagement. FBL is not passive, and it is not a casual networking club. It is a covenant-driven leadership brotherhood.
