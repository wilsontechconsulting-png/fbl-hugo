FBL Cohort Meetings

Join us every 2nd and 4th Tuesday of the month for FBL Cohort meetings. These gatherings are where high-integrity business leaders sharpen one another professionally, personally, and spiritually.

Location: 10087 Old Union Rd suite b, Union, KY 41091
Time: 7:00-8:30 AM (EDT/EST)
Format: In-person

What to Expect

  • Structured accountability and peer leadership
  • Real conversations with experienced operators
  • Faith-integrated business insights
  • Strategic connections and collaboration
  • Coffee, conversation, and real growth

How to Attend

Current Members: RSVP through the Circle community
Prospective Members: Apply for membership to join us
