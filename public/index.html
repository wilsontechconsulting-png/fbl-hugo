What Makes FBL Different

This is not
typical networking.

Yes, you'll meet other leaders.
Yes, there's a membership investment.
But FBL isn't built around collecting contacts—it's built around covenant relationships.

This is a structured environment for growth, accountability, and alignment—where business, leadership, and faith are intentionally integrated.

It's not surface-level. It's not transactional. It's not about what you can get—it's about who you become through real relationships and shared conviction.

Official FBL Mission Statement

Fellowship of Business Leaders unites owners, executives, and professionals dedicated to living out their faith and values through business.

Our mission is to inspire, equip, and mobilize leaders for transformative impact.

Built On

Three Core Pillars

High Trust

Curated membership through application, covenant, and assessment. Protected room = protected value.

High Expectation

Active builders committed to growth through truth, challenge, and real accountability.

High Accountability

Real relationships and ongoing collaboration—not surface-level networking.

Membership Criteria

You belong here if:

  • You're building something (not just thinking)
  • You lead with conviction, not profit
  • You value accountability and truth
  • You're open to challenge

If that's you, we'd like to meet you.
