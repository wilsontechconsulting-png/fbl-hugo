Welcome to Fellowship of Business Leaders

April 22, 2026 • FBL Team

Fellowship of Business Leaders exists because most leadership networks miss the mark.

They’re either too shallow (networking happy hours with business cards), too rigid (corporate consulting dressed up as community), or too disconnected from what actually drives us—faith, family, and the responsibility of leading well.

FBL is different.

What We’re Building

We’re building a high-trust network of business owners, operators, and executives who:

  • Lead with conviction, not consensus
  • Pursue wholeness across faith, work, and life
  • Value accountability over affirmation
  • Execute rather than theorize

This isn’t about scale. It’s about depth.

Who Belongs Here

You belong here if you’re:

  • Running something real (employees, revenue, decisions that matter)
  • Integrating faith into how you lead (not compartmentalizing)
  • Looking for real accountability (not another surface-level network)
  • Willing to contribute, not just consume

What We’re Not

We’re not a church group. We’re not a networking club. We’re not a pay-to-play membership.

We’re a structured environment for growth where business, leadership, and faith are intentionally integrated.

Next Steps

If this resonates, start with the membership process. Read the covenant. Consider the expectations. If you’re in, we’re ready to build with you.
