### 3. ⚠️ MISSING NONDISCRIMINATION STATEMENT

**Bylaws Requirement (Article IX):**
> "The Corporation shall not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, gender, age, disability, or religious affiliation in its programs or activities."

**Website:** No nondiscrimination statement found on any page.

**Issue:** Bylaws require nondiscrimination policy; website should reflect this commitment.

**Recommendation:** Add nondiscrimination statement to:
1. About page (recommended)
2. Membership page (recommended)
3. Terms of Service (recommended)
4. Footer (optional)

**Suggested Language for About Page:**
```markdown
## Equal Opportunity & Nondiscrimination

Fellowship of Business Leaders does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, gender, age, disability, or religious affiliation in its programs, activities, or membership. We welcome all qualified business leaders who align with our mission and values.
```

**Suggested Addition to Membership Page:**
```markdown
### Membership is Open to All Qualified Leaders

FBL does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, gender, age, disability, or religious affiliation. We evaluate applicants based on professional qualifications, character, and alignment with FBL values.
```

**Suggested Addition to Terms of Service (Section 3: Membership):**
```markdown
### Equal Opportunity

FBL does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, gender, age, disability, or religious affiliation in membership decisions or program participation. All qualified applicants are evaluated based on merit, character, and alignment with FBL's mission.
```
