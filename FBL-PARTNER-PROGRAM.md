# FBL Partner Program
## Strategic Business Partner Model

**Created:** April 27, 2026  
**Purpose:** Controlled, valuable access to FBL members without spam or dilution

---

## THE PROBLEM

**Businesses want access to FBL members, but:**
- We can't let anyone contact members freely (spam risk)
- We need to maintain exclusivity and value
- Members pay $150/month for a curated network
- We need to protect the member experience

**Solution:** Tiered Partner Program with strict controls

---

## PARTNER TIERS

### **Tier 1: Community Partner**
**Investment:** $500/month

**What They Get:**
- Listed in Partner Directory (website)
- Logo on Partners page
- Monthly email mention (1 paragraph max)
- Quarterly spotlight in member newsletter
- Access to member-only deals platform
- Attendance at 1 quarterly event per year

**What They DON'T Get:**
- No direct member contact info
- No mass emails to members
- No speaking slots at events
- No promotion in weekly communications

**Best For:** 
- Local service providers
- Professional services (accounting, legal, insurance)
- Software/tools relevant to business owners

**Expected Demand:** High (entry point)

---

### **Tier 2: Strategic Partner**
**Investment:** $2,500/month

**What They Get:**
- Everything in Community Partner, PLUS:
- Featured partner spotlight (homepage rotation)
- 5-minute speaking slot at 1 quarterly event
- Dedicated email to members (1 per quarter, FBL curated)
- Access to member demographics/industries (anonymized)
- Co-branded content opportunities
- Priority placement in Partner Directory

**What They DON'T Get:**
- No direct access to member contact list
- No weekly email slots
- No event sponsorship exclusivity

**Best For:**
- Business coaches/consultants
- Technology platforms
- Financial services
- Marketing agencies

**Expected Demand:** Medium (value seekers)

---

### **Tier 3: Premier Partner**
**Investment:** $7,500/month (or $75,000/year prepaid = 2 months free)

**What They Get:**
- Everything in Strategic Partner, PLUS:
- Exclusive category rights (only 1 per industry vertical)
- Quarterly webinar/training session for members
- Booth at all FBL events (national & local)
- Monthly dedicated email (FBL approved, co-branded)
- Member introductions (opted-in members only, max 10/month)
- Logo on all event materials
- Executive profile in FBL content
- Annual speaking slot at national conference

**What They DON'T Get:**
- No unfettered member access
- All communications still FBL-approved
- No spam privileges

**Best For:**
- Major platforms/enterprise software
- National service providers
- High-value consultancies
- Strategic technology partners

**Expected Demand:** Low (premium positioning)

---

## CONTROLS & PROTECTIONS

### **Member Protection Rules:**

1. **No Direct Contact Without Opt-In**
   - Partners never get raw contact lists
   - All communications go through FBL
   - Members can opt out anytime

2. **Quality Review**
   - All partner content reviewed by FBL leadership
   - Must align with FBL values
   - No hard sales, only value-first

3. **Communication Limits**
   - Community: Max 4 touches/year
   - Strategic: Max 8 touches/year
   - Premier: Max 12 touches/year

4. **Value Requirement**
   - Partners must offer exclusive member benefits
   - Minimum 15% discount or special pricing
   - Must demonstrate ongoing value

5. **Removal Policy**
   - If members complain → warning
   - If spam/abuse → immediate termination
   - No refunds for policy violations

## IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

### **Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-2)**

1. **Create Partner Application Process**
   - Application form on website
   - Review criteria checklist
   - Leadership approval required

2. **Build Partner Directory Page**
   - `/partners` page on fblconnect.com
   - Filterable by tier/industry
   - Clear value statements

3. **Sign First 5 Partners**
   - 3 Community tier (easy wins)
   - 2 Strategic tier (credibility)
   - 0-1 Premier tier (if perfect fit available)

4. **Create Partner Communications Schedule**
   - Editorial calendar
   - Content review workflow
   - Member opt-in/out system

---

### **Phase 2: Growth (Months 3-6)**

1. **Expand Partner Base**
   - Target: 10-15 total partners
   - Focus on diverse industry coverage
   - Maintain quality over quantity

2. **Measure Member Satisfaction**
   - Survey members quarterly
   - Track opt-out rates
   - Adjust program based on feedback

3. **Refine Value Delivery**
   - Track partner benefit usage
   - Ensure partners deliver value
   - Remove underperformers

---

### **Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)**

1. **Launch Premier Tier Exclusivity**
   - Lock in category leaders
   - Annual contracts for stability
   - Premium positioning

2. **Expand Partner Benefits**
   - Event booth opportunities
   - Co-branded content
   - Member introduction programs

3. **Revenue Optimization**
   - Consider annual prepay discounts
   - Test price increases based on value
   - Introduce multi-chapter packages

## PRICING RATIONALE

### **Why These Price Points Work:**

**Community ($500/month):**
- Accessible entry point
- Comparable to low-tier sponsorships
- ROI with just 1-2 member conversions/year
- Easy "yes" for local businesses

**Strategic ($2,500/month):**
- Serious investment signals commitment
- 5x Community = real differentiation
- ROI with 5-10 member conversions/year
- Sweet spot for consultants/agencies

**Premier ($7,500/month):**
- Premium positioning
- 3x Strategic = exclusivity
- ROI with 15-20 conversions/year OR high LTV deals
- Only for enterprises/major players

## PARTNER AGREEMENT TEMPLATE

### **Key Terms:**

1. **Duration:** Month-to-month (auto-renew) or annual prepay
2. **Payment:** Due 1st of month, net 15
3. **Termination:** 30 days notice either party
4. **Content Approval:** All communications require FBL approval
5. **Member Benefit:** Minimum 15% discount or exclusive pricing
6. **No Spam:** Violation = immediate termination
7. **Exclusivity:** Premier partners get category exclusivity
8. **Renewals:** Pricing locked for 12 months, then market rate

## RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### **Risk 1: Partners Don't See ROI**
**Mitigation:** 
- Pre-screen for fit
- Set clear expectations upfront
- Provide engagement data
- Help partners optimize messaging

### **Risk 2: Members Feel Spammed**
**Mitigation:**
- Strict communication limits
- Quality review process
- Easy opt-out
- Regular member surveys

### **Risk 3: Too Many Partners Dilutes Value**
**Mitigation:**
- Cap total partners at 100
- Maintain quality bar
- Premier exclusivity
- Regular partner performance reviews

### **Risk 4: Partners Compete with Members**
**Mitigation:**
- Screen for conflicts of interest
- Favor complementary services
- No direct competitors to member businesses
