## How Partner Access Works

We protect our members' experience while giving partners real value. Here's how:

### Quality Over Quantity

Every partner communication is reviewed by FBL leadership. No spam. No desperate pitching. Only value-driven content that aligns with our mission.

### Controlled Communication Limits

- Founding Partners: 6 touches per year maximum
- Growth Partners: 12-16 touches per year maximum
- Strategic Partners: 12+ touches (monitored closely)

**No member receives more than 2 partner touches per month across all partners.**

### Opt-In Member Introductions

Partners never get member contact lists. Introductions happen only when members opt in and express interest. We protect our members' inboxes.

### Member Benefit Requirements

All partners must offer FBL members exclusive value:
- Minimum 15% discount or special pricing
- Priority service or support
- Exclusive features or early access

**If you can't offer real member value, partnership isn't the right fit.**

## Partner Program FAQs

**How many members does FBL currently have?**

We're in growth phase with 12-15 committed members. Founding Partners lock in pricing now before we scale to 50, 100, or 200+ members.

**What kind of ROI can I expect?**

With 12-15 business owner members, most partners need just 1-2 client conversions per year to achieve positive ROI. As FBL grows, ROI multiplies.

**Can I contact members directly?**

No. All communications go through FBL. Member introductions happen only when members opt in. We protect member experience first.

**What if members don't respond to my offers?**

If you're not seeing engagement, we'll work with you to improve messaging and positioning. But if members consistently opt out, partnership may not be the right fit.

**Can I upgrade from Founding Partner to Growth or Strategic?**

Yes. You can upgrade anytime. Your locked Founding Partner pricing ends when you upgrade, and you pay market rate for the new tier.

**What happens if FBL grows significantly?**

Founding Partners keep locked pricing for 2 years regardless of FBL growth. This is your reward for early belief in the network.

**Do you offer annual prepay discounts?**

Strategic Partners can prepay annually for 2 months free ($20,000/year instead of $24,000). Other tiers are month-to-month only.

**What if I'm not sure which tier is right?**

Apply and we'll have a conversation about your goals, budget, and expected ROI. We'll recommend the tier that makes the most sense.

## Partner Application Process

### Step 1: Submit Application

Tell us about your company, what you offer, how it benefits FBL members, and which tier you're interested in.

FBL leadership evaluates fit based on:

If approved, we'll schedule a call to discuss expectations, communication guidelines, and member benefit requirements.
