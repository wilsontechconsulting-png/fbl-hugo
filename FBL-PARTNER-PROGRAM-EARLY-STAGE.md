## EARLY-STAGE PARTNER TIERS

### **Founding Partner - $150/month**
*"Get in early before it scales"*

**What They Get:**
- Listed as "Founding Partner" on website (permanent badge)
- Logo on Partners page
- Bi-monthly email mention (every other month)
- Annual member event attendance
- **Locked-in pricing for 2 years** (even as FBL grows)
- First access to member deals/offers

**Limit:** Maximum 10 Founding Partners (scarcity creates value)

**Why This Works:**
- Same price as ONE member ($150)
- Access to 12-15 decision-makers
- Price locks for 2 years (huge value if FBL grows to 100+ members)
- "Founding Partner" status has prestige value

**Best For:**
- Local businesses wanting credibility
- Service providers testing the waters
- Companies betting on FBL growth

---

### **Growth Partner - $500/month**
*"More access, more value"*

**What They Get:**
- Everything in Founding Partner, PLUS:
- Monthly email feature (every month vs every other)
- 5-minute speaking slot at quarterly event
- Co-branded content opportunity (1 per quarter)
- Member intro to 3-5 opted-in members per quarter
- Featured partner spotlight (homepage)

**Limit:** Maximum 5 Growth Partners

**Why This Works:**
- $33/member/month for direct access
- Speaking opportunities = credibility
- Member intros = actual leads
- Still affordable, more benefits

**Best For:**
- Business coaches/consultants
- High-value service providers
- Companies with clear member benefit

---

### **Strategic Partner - $1,500/month**
*"Premium access, category exclusivity"*

**What They Get:**
- Everything in Growth Partner, PLUS:
- **Category exclusivity** (only 1 per industry vertical)
- Quarterly webinar/training for members
- Monthly dedicated email (co-branded with FBL)
- Booth at all FBL events
- Member intros to ALL interested members (unlimited)
- Executive profile in FBL communications

**Limit:** Maximum 3 Strategic Partners

**Why This Works:**
- $100/member/month for exclusive access
- Category exclusivity = competitive advantage
- Training members = relationship building
- Unlimited intros = real pipeline

**Best For:**
- Premium service providers
- Technology platforms
- Major consultancies willing to invest early

## RISKS & MITIGATIONS (EARLY STAGE)

### **Risk: Partners don't see value with small member base**
**Mitigation:**
- Set realistic expectations (1-2 conversions/year)
- Emphasize locked pricing upside
- Help partners create valuable member offers
- Track and report engagement

### **Risk: Too many partners overwhelm 12-15 members**
**Mitigation:**
- Cap at 10 total partners max
- Strict communication limits
- Quality review all content
- Monitor member feedback closely

### **Risk: Members feel spammed**
**Mitigation:**
- 2 touches/month max per member total
- Easy opt-out
- High-value content only
- Remove bad actors immediately
