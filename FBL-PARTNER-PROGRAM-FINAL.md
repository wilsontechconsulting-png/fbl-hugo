# FBL Partner Program - Final Model
## Strategic Pricing Above Membership

**Created:** April 27, 2026  
**Current Members:** 12-15  
**Member Price:** $150/month  
**Partner Logic:** Premium above membership (marketing access = premium)

---

## STRATEGIC PRICING PRINCIPLE

**Members pay $150/month for:**
- Network access
- Event attendance
- Peer support
- Learning opportunities

**Partners pay MORE than $150/month for:**
- Marketing access to members
- Promotional opportunities
- Brand positioning
- Lead generation

**Key insight:** If you just want network access → Become a member  
**If you want to MARKET → Pay the partner premium**

---

## PARTNER TIERS (ABOVE MEMBERSHIP)

### **Founding Partner - $250/month**
*66% premium above membership*

**What They Get:**
- Listed as "Founding Partner" on website (permanent)
- Logo on Partners page
- Bi-monthly email mention (6 per year)
- Annual member event attendance
- **Locked pricing for 2 years** (even as FBL grows)
- Member-exclusive discount requirement (min 15%)

**Why $250 makes sense:**
- Clear premium above $150 membership
- Still very accessible ($100 more than joining)
- Forces decision: "Just join?" vs "Market to members?"
- Locked pricing = great early-bird value

**Limit:** 10 Founding Partners max

**ROI calculation:**
- $250/month = $3,000/year
- Need 1 client at $5K+ for positive ROI
- With 12-15 business owners, very achievable

---

### **Growth Partner - $750/month**
*5x membership, serious commitment*

**What They Get:**
- Everything in Founding Partner, PLUS:
- Monthly email feature (12 per year vs 6)
- 5-minute speaking slot at quarterly events
- Co-branded content (1 per quarter)
- Member introductions (3-5 opted-in per quarter)
- Featured homepage spotlight

**Why $750 makes sense:**
- 3x the Founding Partner price
- $600 premium above membership
- Signals serious intent
- More touches = more value = higher price

**Limit:** 5 Growth Partners max

**ROI calculation:**
- $750/month = $9,000/year
- Need 2 clients at $5K+ for positive ROI
- Speaking + intros + monthly email = real pipeline

---

### **Strategic Partner - $2,000/month**
*Premium positioning, category exclusive*

**What They Get:**
- Everything in Growth Partner, PLUS:
- **Category exclusivity** (only 1 per industry)
- Quarterly member webinar/training
- Monthly dedicated email (co-branded)
- Event booth at all FBL events
- Unlimited member introductions (opt-in)
- Executive profile in FBL content

**Why $2,000 makes sense:**
- 13x membership price
- Category exclusivity = competitive moat
- Unlimited intros = serious lead gen
- Premium investment for premium return

**Limit:** 3 Strategic Partners max

**ROI calculation:**
- $2,000/month = $24,000/year
- Need 3-5 clients at $5K+ for positive ROI
- OR 1-2 clients at $15K+ lifetime value

## FOUNDING PARTNER PITCH (UPDATED)

**For companies considering partner program:**

> "FBL membership is $150/month - that gets you network access and peer support.
>
> But if you want to MARKET to our members, Founding Partners pay $250/month - just $100 more.
>
> Here's the deal: That $250 is LOCKED for 2 years. When we grow to 50, 100, or 200 members, you're still at $250 while new partners pay $500-$10,000.
>
> You get permanent 'Founding Partner' status, bi-monthly exposure to highly engaged business owners, and first-mover advantage.
>
> We're only taking 10 Founding Partners. After that, entry is $500+ minimum.
>
> If you just want the network, join as a member for $150. If you want marketing access with locked pricing, it's $250.
>
> Which makes more sense for you?"

**Why this pitch works:**
- Clear comparison ($150 member vs $250 partner)
- Forces them to choose their intent
- Locked pricing creates urgency
- Scarcity (only 10 spots)

## MEMBER PROTECTION (CRITICAL)

**With only 12-15 members, strict limits:**

### **Communication Caps:**

**Founding Partner:**
- Bi-monthly mentions = 6/year
- Event attendance = 1/year
- **Total: 7 touches/year max**

**Growth Partner:**
- Monthly mentions = 12/year
- Speaking slots = 4/year
- Member intros = 4/year
- **Total: 20 touches/year max**

**Strategic Partner:**
- Monthly emails = 12/year
- Quarterly webinars = 4/year
- Member intros = unlimited (track closely)
- **Total: 16+ direct touches**

**Global Rule:** No member receives more than 2 partner touches per month across ALL partners combined.
